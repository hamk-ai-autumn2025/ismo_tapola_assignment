About Us

We are a team of scientists, engineers, and visionaries dedicated to unlocking the power of nature. Our mission is to replace fossil-based products with sustainable, bio-based alternatives that are both economically viable and environmentally friendly.

Our Products

BioPolymers

High-performance, biodegradable polymers for packaging, textiles, and 3D printing, derived from plant-based starches and agricultural waste.

Advanced Biofuels

Next-generation liquid fuels produced from non-food biomass, offering a clean, carbon-neutral alternative for transportation and energy sectors.

Bio-based Chemicals

Sustainable chemical building blocks for cosmetics, agriculture, and industrial applications, replacing petrochemical-based ingredients.

Our Services

We offer research and development partnerships, sustainability consulting, and custom biotechnology solutions to help your business transition to a circular, bio-based economy.
